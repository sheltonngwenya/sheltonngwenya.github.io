3 DAY SPLIT ROUTINE

REMEMBER TECHNIQUE, TECHNIQUE, TECHNIQUE!!!

IMPROPER TECHNIQUE LEADS TO INJURIES

Click the video below and follow Athlean-X and get professional guidance

Day 1: Chest. Biceps, abs

* 4 sets of flat alternating push-ups (10x wide hand position, 10x normal, 10x triangle)

* 4 sets of dumbell bicep curls (standing, both arms simultaneously) try get to between 10 and 15 reps on each set

* 2 sets of mixed stomach crunches (10x postion flat on your back, legs bent and feet flat on the floor, 10x legs raised up so that your calves are parallel to the floor, 10x legs straight up, feet towards the sky then 10x half way down again and 10x feet flat on the floor to finish)

* 3 sets of incline push-ups (Feet elevated on a chair, hands on the floor) 20 reps per set

* 3 sets of standing hammer-grip dumbell curls (alternating this time, try get to 12 reps per hand per set)

* 3 sets of hanging leg raises (Hang on a pull-up bar and raise your knees up into your chest, then lower them back down to the start position) aim for 20 reps each set

* 3 sets of decline push-ups (hands resting on a chair or bench this time feet still on the floor) 20 reps per set

* 3 sets reverse grip pull-ups (reverse grip to allow bicep concentration, aim to get your chin just above the bar each time) 10-15 reps per set

* 2 sets of side plank (lie on you side with your elbow supporting your weight, lift your body up so that just the rear part of your fore-arm and your feet are touching the floor. The rest of your body suspended.) hold on each side for 1 minute

Day 2: Back. Triceps

* 4 sets of wide grip pull-ups (aim for 10-15 reps per set with your chin above the bar on each rep)

* 4 sets of hanging tricep dips (between 2 chairs or tables, support your body with your hands on each side, raise your legs behind you so that they are parallel to the floor, then dip down until your chest is in line with your hands and push back up to starting position) aim for 15-20 reps per set.

* 4 sets of single arm dumbell rows (rest your hand on a chair and keep your body bent over so that your back is almost parallel to the floor) Aim for 15-20 reps per side

* 4 sets of dumbell overhead tricep extensions (one hand at a time, aim for 10-15 reps per hand)

* 4 sets of bent over rear dumbell rows (hand position should be outwards as though you were holding a straight bar) 20 reps per set

* 4 sets of dumbell tricep kick-backs (once again rest your hand on a chair or bench for support) 15 reps per side

* 4 sets of dumbell dead-lift (20 reps per set)

Day 3: Shoulders and legs

* 4 sets of seated or standing dumbell shoulder press (12-20 reps per set)

* 4 sets of dumbell side lateral raises (10-15 reps per set)

* 4 sets of alternating dumbell front delt raises (10-15 reps per side)

* 4 sets of upright dumbell delt rows (10-15 reps per set)

* 4 sets of jump squats (aim to get your legs as high as possible on each rep) 15-20 reps per set

* 4 sets of wide stance sumo squats with dumbells (15-20 reps per set)

* 4 sets of walking weighted lunges. (10 reps per leg)

* 4 sets of close stance deep squats ( 15-20 reps per set)

* 5 sets of weighted standing calve press (20-30 reps per set)
